Praetor VR Demos

And what people get to experience!

What comes in Praetor VR is more than plain hardware. While you do get custom affordable, and even DIY style VR HMDs, Controllers and even haptic devices, it isn't limited to just that. Bringing in the custom built 3D engine for VR, you can now create your own VR experiences with ease!

These are the demonstrations made with Praetor VR for educational and entertainment purposes. Select people have experienced these demos at specific events, and mass testing of demos was also done in classrooms!

'The Lunar Degree'

Educational demo regarding space and future of Moon missions

Highlights:
• 80+ Students have experienced the demo.
• Features integrated AI for answering queries.
• Runs using custom 3D engine designed for VR.
• With Chandrayaan 3 landing, future concepts.
• Mass testing conducted at educational events!.

'Museum in my Mind'

Educational demo to make distant museums closer

Highlights:
• Experience faraway museums in VR.
• With AI Tour Guides.
• Full scale museum recreations for better immersion.
• 50+ Students have experienced the demo.
• Featuring the Hintze Hall, Charterhouse Great Chamber, and more!
This demo is a concept which lets students visit distant museums, allowing cultural visits which would have been far-away earlier!

'Premade Apps Demo'

Got non-VR apps? Don't worry.

Highlights:
• Using special renderer modifications, turn any* app into a VR experience!
*(Supports DirectX 9,10,11,12, and OpenGL, Vulkan exclusively.).
• Runs using custom scripts, and direct VR API.
• Allows to use Windows Apps such as even Word, Excel, etc. in VR!
• Tested apps include games, educational apps, etc..
